Welcome to Your Career Journey

The animation, gaming, and digital art industries offer diverse career paths for talented 3D artists. This resource hub provides you with essential tools, connections, and knowledge to successfully transition from student to professional. Whether you're interested in feature films, game development, advertising, or emerging technologies like VR/AR, these resources will help you navigate the industry landscape.
