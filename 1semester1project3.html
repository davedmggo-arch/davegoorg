Project 3: Interior Design (Weeks 7-8)

You will design an "acting space" interior that supports camera movement and utilizes procedural materials and advanced lighting to tell a story.

Start Learning!!

Project Brief

Goal: You are tasked with designing an interior room that serves as an "acting space." The room must be designed with camera movement and character placement in mind, using procedural materials and advanced lighting to create a specific mood.

Deliverables:

  • 1 Blender File: FirstName_LastName_ANI106_Project_003_v001.blend
  • 3 JPEGs: FirstName_LastName_ANI106_Project_003_v001_001-003.jpg

Marking Rubric (Total: 100 Points):

  • Architectural Blocking (25 pts): Proper use of real-world scale and furniture arrangement to create functional paths.
  • Procedural Materials (25 pts): Complexity and quality of node-based shaders for floors, walls, and furniture.
  • Interior Lighting (25 pts): Use of Area lights for soft daylight and Point/Spot lights for practical interior sources.
  • Cinematography (25 pts): Planning of multiple camera angles and effective use of focal length and depth of field.

Week 7
