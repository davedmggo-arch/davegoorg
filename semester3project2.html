Project 2: "The Resident" (Weeks 5-8)

You will create a simple character that would inhabit or use the space and objects from your previous project. The character should be designed without dynamic fur or hair, using a relatively low polygon count suitable for beginners to sculpt. Focus on creating a strong silhouette and clear form that communicates the character's personality and function within your chosen theme and target audience. You will learn about character components - the individual elements that combine to create a cohesive character design.

Deliverables

Deliverables:
  • Three blocked-out concept sculpts in Blender
  • Refined sculpt of one chosen concept.
  • Fully UV unwrapped and textured version of the chosen sculpt.
  • One .blend file containing concepts, chosen sculpt, textures, and rig.
  • Naming convention: FirstName_LastName_Project_2_ANV306.blend

Character Options:

1. Children’s Fantasy — “Enchanted Tea Party”
A moonlit sunroom where characters enjoy cookies and stories.
The Storyteller Child: Pajama-clad child with a story to tell.
Enchanted Tea Host: Miniature host with floating teacup and whimsical attire.
Moonlit Toy Companion: A plush toy magically brought to life.

2. Teen Sci-Fi — “Vigilante Detective’s Workshop”
A bedroom/workshop where a teen vigilante 3D-prints gadgets to fight corruption.
The Gadgeteer Teen: Hooded teen with mechanical gauntlets and mask.
AI Drone Companion: Small floating robot built from scrap and 3D-printed parts.
Corrupt City Informant: Masked teen with trench coat and hacked tech.

3. Young Adult Urban — “Teen Ghost Hunter”
A haunted bedroom where a teen sets up paranormal gear.
The Ghost Hunter Teen: Street-smart teen with EMF reader and camera rig.
Spectral Apparition: Translucent ghost with wispy, uncanny features.
The Paranormal Tech Rig: Contraption of glowing sensors and hacked-together gear.

4. Adult Historical — “Masked Thief’s Attic”
A 1900s attic drafting table with maps, plants, and stolen clippings.
The Masked Thief: Agile figure with stitched mask, grappling hook, and blueprints.
The Informant: Historical ally with monocle, coded messages, and lantern.
The Attic Crow: Intelligent bird with satchel, perched in the rafters.
