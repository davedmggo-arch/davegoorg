Project 2: Basic Character (Weeks 4-6)

You are tasked with designing a basic character bust and transforming it from a high-detail sculpt into a functional, rigged asset capable of performing a simple animation.

Start Learning!!

Project Brief

Goal: You will design and create a basic character bust (head, torso, and arms). The goal is to move from a high-detail artistic sculpt to a functional, rigged 3D asset that can perform a simple animation.

Deliverables:

  • 1 Blender File: FirstName_LastName_ANI106_Project_002_v001.blend
  • 3 JPEGs: FirstName_LastName_ANI106_Project_002_v001_001-003.jpg

Marking Rubric (Total: 100 Points):

  • Sculpting & Proportions (25 pts): Mastery of digital clay tools to define organic silhouettes and anatomical landmarks.
  • Retopology (25 pts): Technical accuracy of the low-poly mesh and placement of edge loops for smooth facial movement.
  • Rigging & Weighting (25 pts): Correct placement of bone chains and successful skin weighting to the mesh.
  • Animation & Rendering (25 pts): Quality of the final performance (e.g., head turn) and successful baking of detail maps.

Featured Courses

Week 4

You will begin sculpting "digital clay" to master organic proportions and block out the expressive forms of your character’s head.

Week 5

You will expand your sculpt to the torso and arms before performing retopology to create a clean mesh that the computer can easily animate.

Week 6

You will transform your model into a living character by applying custom textures and recording a short performance through keyframing.
