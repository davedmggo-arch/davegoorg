Build a Starter Wall Kit
  • Base wall segment: Plane 2 m Ã— 2 m â†’ Extrude thickness 0.2 m; Bevel edges; UV map.
  • Corner piece: duplicate base; shape into L; ensure both edges align; keep dims/connection points.
  • Doorway piece: duplicate base; cut opening; maintain outer alignment; add simple trim.
  • Floor & ceiling: flat pieces matching wall span; align to wall tops/bottoms; prep textures.
  • Variations: duplicate types and add subtle details/damage while preserving connection points.
  • Test the kit: assemble a small room; check for gaps/misalignments and fix.
  • Build a simple structure with the kit (room or corridor) to validate the system.
QA Checklist
  • Do all pieces adhere to standard size and connection points?
  • Do tiles/modules snap seamlessly with no gaps?
  • Is there enough variation to avoid repetition?
  • Did you test connections early and often?
