Semester 4: Exterior World Expansion

Move beyond the "room" and into the "world." You will master modularity, technical optimization, and atmospheric storytelling.

Start Learning!!

Featured Courses

Project 1: Environmental Tiles and Procedural Systems (Weeks 1-3)

Designing modular tiles that function as "kit pieces" for a larger world. Environment Options: Forest, Shoreline, or Mountain scenery. The Technical Core: You will master weight painting as a data source and use the Blender 4.5 Vulkan backend for high fidelity real time feedback.

Project 2: The Narrative Inhabitant (Weeks 4-8)

Focus: Creating an "Animation Ready" character that physically belongs in your Project 1 world. Constraints: Strictly under 5,000 polygons, no fur or hair, and a functional body rig. The Technical Core: We will utilize the new Blender 4.5 Retopology Overlays to ensure your mesh flow (topology) is industry standard.

Project 3: The Cinematic Horizon (Weeks 9-12)

Focus: Atmospheric storytelling and professional portfolio packaging. Goal: Use matte paintings and skyboxes to create an illusion of infinite scale. The Technical Core: Mastering Blender 4.5’s Exposure and Temperature controls to simulate realistic photography.
